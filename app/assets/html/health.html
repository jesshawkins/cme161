Control Center

Use the pie chart of number of steps to filter the subject. Use the slider to change the dependent variable.










Hourly Statistics

You can also filter the data by hour of day.






















Daily Statistics

You can also filter the data by day.






















Weekly Statistics

You can also filter the data by day of week.






















Composite Graphic

This is a composite graphic that captures several variables. You can drag and zoom in the chart to explore.

X Axis = Number of steps
Y Axis = Number of active calories
Radius = Distance walked (km)
Color = Average heart rate






















Bonus Graphic

This chart looks at the relationship between number of steps and the difference between resting and active heart rate over the hours of the day.
